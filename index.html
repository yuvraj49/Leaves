WHAT ARE LEAVES ?

Leaves produce oxygen by taking in carbon-dioxide present in the atmosphere by Artificial Photosynthesis .

It carefully measures the amount of CO2 present in the surrounding environment,absorbs them and provide pure oxygen.

Leaves are integrated with AI controlled pixel lens covering the surface of pods which detects the direction of sunrays falling on each pixel and align themselves thereby magnifing the rays it to core thus generating maximum efficiency.

The core consists of efficient electrodes and aiONE chip which processes artificial photosynthesis when a certain amount of sun rays reaches to the core thereby preforming photosynthesis exactly as the plants do.
